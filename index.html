About UniExplorer

UniExplorer is a comprehensive digital platform designed to simplify the tertiary education application process for South African students. Our tool bridges the gap between high school completion and university admission by providing a centralized hub where students can discover, evaluate, and apply to university programmes that match their academic profile and career aspirations.

The platform currently aggregates programme information from all 24 public universities across South Africa, with plans to expand to include private institutions and TVET colleges. We present detailed admission requirements, APS score calculations, subject prerequisites, and programme durations in an easy-to-understand format. Students can input their matric results to instantly see which programmes they qualify for, eliminating the guesswork and reducing application anxiety.

Beyond just programme discovery, UniExplorer serves as a personal education assistant, allowing students to save their favorite programmes, track application deadlines, and access direct links to university application portals. Our mission is to democratize access to higher education information and empower every South African student to make informed decisions about their academic future.
